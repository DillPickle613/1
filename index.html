Information About Organ Trafficking

Organ trafficking is the illegal use, transportation, and gathering of human organs. In China,this is a 1 billion dollar business. Although it might generate lots of money for China, it is extremely cruel and harmful to everyone. Every year, China illegally carries out over 60,000 organ transplants. Most of these come from people who don't agree with China's political stance, or religion. These people are killed based on their beliefs and executed by the government and used for illegal organ transplants.

Evidence of Organ Trafficking in China

There is lots of evidence to support the theory that China is engaging in this. A book published in 2009 called "Bloody Harvest", goes in- depth onn these killings. In the book, a phone call recorded states this:

Caller: I want to know how long [the patients] have to wait [for a liver transplant].

Dr. Dai: The supply of organs we have, we have every day. We do them every day.

Caller: We want fresh, live ones.

Dr. Dai: They are all live, all live…

Caller: I heard some come from those who practice Falun Gong, those who are very healthy.

Dr. Dai: Yes, we have. I can’t talk openly to you over the phone.

Caller: If you can find me this type, I am coming very soon.

Dr. Dai: It’s OK. Please come.

This coverstation is between a chinese doctor and a unspecified caller. The doctor admits that he does organ transplants everyday, and gets them everyday. Later in the conversation, the doctor said that some of these are from Falun Gong Practioners. The only way they can get so many organs that fast, is by killing these people. Thats what their doing to these practioners. They get all of these organs from imprisoning and killing the Falun Gong practioners.
